# Bloody Roar II fight HUD observation (2026-07-09)

This is a narrative receipt, not a preserved raw telemetry log.

On the TrimUI Brick, the v1.3 candidate was parked in Bloody Roar II character-select and
fight scenes with the MinArch debug HUD visible. After the GPU-thread and scaler fixes, the
HUD reported 60 generated frames per 60 target frames while the governor provisioned up to
the 1800 MHz stock ceiling. The same session reported zero ALSA underruns across the observed
window, including one deliberate failed 1008 MHz probe and immediate BIGSLIP recovery.

The contemporaneous engineering record is `docs/DECISIONS.md` D48 and its second addendum.

raw data.
